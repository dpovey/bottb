About the Event

Sydney Tech Battle brings together the most talented musicians from the tech industry for an unforgettable night of rock, metal, and everything in between. Eight bands will take the stage at the iconic Metro Theatre, competing for glory and bragging rights.

From software engineers to product managers, from startups to tech giants — when the workday ends, the music begins. This is where passion projects become center stage performances.
