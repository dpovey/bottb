- Battle of the tech bands Videographer Brief \*

Battle of the tech bands is a community charity music event held annually. The event involve 4-6 bands competing live on stage by playing their own cover songs. Battle of the tech bands has run in Brisbane for 5 years, and recently has expanded to Sydney and Melbourne.

With photography and videography, our goal is to make the bands "feel like rock stars", but also to support up and coming photographers and videographers. We want your creativity and originality to shine through not be a sterile corporate promotion. We want you to be on stage, and in the thick of the crowd, capturing the energy and vibe of the event.

As a charity event we try to keep costs low, and we are sometimes limited by what sponsorhsip is able to provide. As such we are looking for a range of options for the service you can provide. Please indicate what price you would be willing to provide for each service.

For equipment, we are able to hire additional at your recommendation, however this will factor into our decision for the final pricing.

Timeliness is really important to us. We use videos to promote future events, and the value of these declines with the proximity to the last one. We include timelines below, while these are negotiable up-front, if you fail to deliver by an agreed timeline we will reduce payment by 20%? (what is reasonable standard here).

For you we also want to do our best to support local artists, we promote photographers and videographers on our website, and for instagram posts we can include you as a collaborator. We can also @mention you on linked in for video posts.

The brisbane event at the triffid typically attracts 400-500 people.

Event:
Thursday 27th of August, at the triffid. Doors open 6:30pm, Soundcheck is from around 2-3pm usually. Event usually finishes around 11pm
There are 5 bands of 25-30 minutes sets typically with 10m between. There will be some mc remarks and remarks from sponsors and charity to capture as well. As well as the closing award presentation.
